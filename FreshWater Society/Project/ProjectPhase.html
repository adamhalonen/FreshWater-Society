News Feed

Success!

This past week we finished a huge rain garden for the city of St. Paul; with the help of University of St. Thomas Students

Thank You Volunteers

This past year we have seen the highest turnout of volunteers to help with projects, because of this we have been able to complete more work then ever. Thank you so much for your help!

Finished the Rain Garden at 2115 Summit ave

This past weekend we finished constructing a garden at 2115 Summit ave, this rain garden should eliminate 50 gallons of water runoff this year alone

Another Watershed District

We have expanded to yet another watershed district!


What's this about?

This is The Master Water Stewards program, a program that certifies and supports community leaders to install pollution prevention projects that educate community members, reduce pollutants from polluted runoff, and allow more water to soak into the ground before running into storm sewer systems. Wherever you live, you can make a real difference in the health of our waters.
